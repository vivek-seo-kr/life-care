A clean, fast, and professional single-page portfolio website for **Life Care Clinic**, led by Dr. (Col) S Mandal, a retired Indian Army Medical Corps officer and MD Medicine Specialist in Dharamshala, Himachal Pradesh.

## About

This portfolio is designed to help patients find and connect with Life Care Clinic online. It highlights Dr. (Col) S Mandal's expertise in evidence-based medicine and the clinic's comprehensive healthcare services, making it easy for patients to book appointments and get directions to the clinic in Dharamshala.

## Sections

- **Hero** — Introduction with clinic name and call-to-action
- **About** — Dr. Mandal's background and Army Medical Corps experience
- **Services** — Medical treatments and diagnostic services offered
- **Testimonials** — Patient reviews and feedback
- **FAQ** — Common queries about services and appointments
- **Contact** — Address, phone, and Google Maps location
- **Footer** — Clinic info and branding

4. Your site will be live at `https://your-username.github.io/repo-name`
